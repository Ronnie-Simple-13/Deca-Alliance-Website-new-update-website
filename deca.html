About Our School

Deca Alliance Secondary School was founded in 2017 with the mission to provide quality education to all students regardless of their background. Our motto "Education is a necessity" reflects our belief that education is the foundation for personal and societal development.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced teachers, and a comprehensive curriculum, we strive to nurture well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of the modern world.

Our school offers both arts and science programs, day and boarding secondary School with various extracurricular activities to help students discover and develop their talents.
